This online recipe platform features a modern and simple design. It uses card components and other UI elements based on a set design system, which includes specific colors, fonts, and components.

The design includes a sidebar with filters to help you find recipes by categories like cooking time and more. It also has a "Recipe of the Day" section and various food categories for users to explore.
