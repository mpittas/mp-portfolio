This is a project for fundraiser campaigns related to animals. A concept idea developed from my deep love to all animals.

For the design I chose Ivory color since it gives a warm feeling and welcomeness. The accent color is Orange and it sticks out nicely. I chose a serif font because it pairs nicely with the body text.
